Traditional Home The Usual Way We Live In a traditional home, everything is done by hand. You switch on the lights using a button, you lock the doors with a key, and you control your fan or AC manually. Even small things like opening curtains or turning off appliances you have to do it on your own. There’s no remote control, no phone app, and no voice commands. You have to be present at home to control things.

We offer modernised, smart, and interactive homes giving the owners accessibility to their homes with a touch of a button. This website will allow potential customers to have the ability to browse for their new smart home that they can purchase through the website with prebuilt in smart features. The website will contain detailed descriptions of the hardware installed in the home to make the home more digital and interactive with guides and manuals to help first time smart homeowners. Customers will also have a choice to build a new home from our skeleton houses (Homes that haven’t been modernised) and choose what would they like to add on the home such as access control for the doors, CCTV for monitoring, AI assistant that is built into the home entertainment system, solar power or inverter with batteries. The website will give the customer a wide range of options to choose from to suit their needs.
